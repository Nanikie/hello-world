    Introducing the ICT & Business VacWork Program

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to ensure that there are initiatives that address research, innovation, development and economic activities in the Mpumalanga region.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to become Mpumalanga leading intellectual Innovation Hub in creation, development and deployment of research and innovation technologies.

    Our Objective

    Offer young researchers a possibility to network, brainstorm, discuss ideas and engage in collaborative projects developed with other peers from one region

    Program

    Program Overview

    We live in a growing world with different needs and dynamics, in order for us to survive and save the next generation, we need to understand and prepare for what is to come tomorrow.

    One of the greatest questions we have to ask ourselves is: are we ready? Skills capacity has been identified as one of the greatest challenges for South Africa.
    The Geekulcha VacWork program takes in a number of high scholars to a real and co-creative working environment, get trained and build projects with ICT tools.

    Youngsters spend their Holidays With The Geeks. The Geekulcha crew and community get to redistribute their skills/knowledge to the young ones.

    Theme

    Space Science and Technologies

    • arrow_down Theme Breakdown 1

      This edition is centered on Integrated Green Youth Economy challenge young people in taking interest and rally behind the green economy and technologies. Youngsters will use ICT tools to bring the application of science into being and help others adapt and find value.

      The youth being the leaders of the future, it’s up to them to dream and build the future that they want to see tomorrow. How do you accelerate the Green Economy using integrate technologies ?

      At the end of the programme, the participants must see more value of the space programmes in South Africa and explore a socio-economic value out of the solutions.
