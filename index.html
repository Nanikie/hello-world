Introducing the ICT & Business VacWork Programme​

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that there are initiatives that address research, innovation, development and economic activities in the Mpumalanga region.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become Mpumalanga leading intellectual Innovation Hub in creation, development and deployment of research and innovation technologies.

Our Objective

Offer young researchers a possibility to network, brainstorm, discuss ideas and engage in collaborative projects developed with other peers in the region.

Program

Program Overview

We live in a growing world with different needs and dynamics, in order for us to survive and save the next generation, we need to understand and prepare for what is to come tomorrow.

One of the greatest questions we have to ask ourselves is: are we ready? Skills capacity has been identified as one of the greatest challenges for South Africa.
The Geekulcha VacWork program takes in a number of high scholars to a real and co-creative working environment, get trained and build projects with ICT tools.

Youngsters spend their Holidays With The Geeks. The Geekulcha crew and community get to redistribute their skills/knowledge to the young ones.
